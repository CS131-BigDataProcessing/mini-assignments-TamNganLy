a. What other types of tasks could benefit from automation using cron or at?
System Updates: Schedule automated updates for packages or system software, ensuring that the system remains up-to-date.

b. What potential risks are there with poorly managed automated tasks, and how can you avoid them?
Potential risks:
Overwriting Critical Data: Misconfigured tasks could overwrite valuable data. 
How to avoid:
Use unique filenames for backups and maintain version history.
